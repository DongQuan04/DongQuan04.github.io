### Week 7 objectives:

* Practice creating, configuring, and managing EC2 Instances.
* Monitor and evaluate the performance and status of resources on AWS.
* Study the object storage model and how to manage data with Amazon S3.

* Became proficient in creating and configuring **EC2 Instances** with AMI, Instance type, Storage, and Security Group options.
