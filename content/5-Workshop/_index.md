#### Connect and Run the Flutter Application

In this section, you will configure the Flutter application to connect to the backend you deployed in the previous chapter. You will then test the complete business workflow for both application roles:

- **Merchant** — Register a merchant and manage the menu.
- **Customer** — Scan a table QR code, place an order, and complete the payment process.
