#### Connect and Run the Flutter Application

In this section, you will configure the Flutter application to connect to the backend deployed in the previous chapter. You will then execute the complete business workflow for both supported roles:

- **Merchant** — Register a merchant, manage the menu, and process customer orders.
- **Customer** — Scan a table QR code, browse the menu, place an order, and complete the payment.
