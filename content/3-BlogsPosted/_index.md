This section lists and introduces the blogs you have published on the [AWS Study Group](https://www.facebook.com/groups/awsstudygroupfcj). For example:

### [Blog 1 - AUTOMATING MEDICAL RECORD DIGITIZATION WITH AMAZON BEDROCK DATA AUTOMATION & AWS HEALTHLAKE](3.1-Blog1/)
This blog introduces how Generative AI (via Amazon Bedrock Data Automation) can be combined to extract data from unstructured medical documents, then standardize and store it according to the FHIR standard on AWS HealthLake, enabling different hospital systems to interoperate on a fully Serverless architecture.

### [Blog 2 - PHYSICAL AI IN HEALTHCARE: REDEFINING CARE DELIVERY AT THE EDGE](3.2-Blog2/)
This blog introduces AWS, NVIDIA, and AI-SENSE's Physical AI project in the field of home healthcare, through two applications, Virtual Ward and Health Buddy, combining edge computing, Digital Twin simulation, and next-generation connectivity networks to extend the scope of healthcare beyond the hospital.

### [Blog 3 - AUTOMATING LANDING ZONE SETUP IN HOURS WITH AWS TRANSFORM (AI-POWERED)](3.3-Blog3/)
This blog reviews the AWS Transform tool, which enables building a Well-Architected-compliant Landing Zone in just a few hours instead of 4-12 weeks, thanks to an AI Agent that helps propose architectures and generate CDK/LZA code, while still retaining a Human-In-The-Loop mechanism to ensure Admin control.
