Throughout my internship and participation in the AWS Cloud Bootcamp, I had the opportunity to attend intensive technology events. These were not only memorable learning experiences to gather practical knowledge from industry experts, but also valuable opportunities that broadened my system design mindset and shaped my future career orientation.

---

### 1. [Event 1: FCAJ Community Day - Day 1](4.1-Event1/)

* **Event Name:** FCAJ Community Day 2026 (Day 1)
* **Date & Time:** 09:00 AM - 12:00 PM, May 23, 2026
* **Location:** 26th Floor, AWS Vietnam Office (Ho Chi Minh City)
* **Your Role:** Attendee
* **Brief Description:** The sessions focused on core mental models when interacting with Large Language Models (LLMs), the importance of context system management, comprehensive network infrastructure optimization with Amazon CloudFront, and practical real-world lessons under intense pressure from a Hackathon project (LotusHacks).
* **Key Takeaways & Value:**
    * Grasped the *Context-driven Design* philosophy and learned how to build a long-term memory system for AI rather than relying solely on prompt optimization.
    * Approached knowledge regarding network security, handling traffic loads, and cost optimization at the edge via CloudFront.
    * Absorbed the spirit of teamwork and rapid product delivery under immense time constraints.

### 2. [Event 2: FCAJ Community Day - Day 2](4.2-Event2/)

* **Event Name:** FCAJ Community Day 2026 (Day 2) - AI Wave & System Automation
* **Date & Time:** 09:00 AM - 11:30 AM, May 24, 2026
* **Location:** 26th Floor, AWS Vietnam Office (Ho Chi Minh City)
* **Your Role:** Attendee
* **Brief Description:** On that day, I was able to delve deeper into and expand my understanding of enterprise-grade automation solutions, including: Autonomous incident remediation (Deep Response Engine), a 24/7 AI-driven DevOps operations teammate, human-like voice agents using Amazon Nova Sonic, advanced digital HR analytics via Amazon Quick, and securing data connection endpoints using the Model Context Protocol (MCP) private network protocol.
* **Key Takeaways & Value:**
    * Shifted the operational mindset from being alert-driven to proactively action-driven.
    * Mastered the streaming speech-to-speech architecture designed to optimize real-time latency for next-generation conversational AI voice agents.
    * Gained a profound awareness of cloud security compliance, recognizing that isolating AI connections via private VPC Endpoints is mandatory when interacting with sensitive internal enterprise data.

---
> **Summary:** The event series provided a holistic view stretching from core traditional Cloud infrastructure to modern AI-driven application architectures, equipping me with a better foundation for both my upcoming university course projects and actual engineering workflows in the enterprise.
